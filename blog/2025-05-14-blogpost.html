[Placeholder] Day 2: Iconic Paris Landmarks

May 14, 2025

Morning at the Louvre

[Placeholder] Started the day early at the Louvre Museum. The glass pyramid was even more impressive in person, and the museum's collection was breathtaking. The Mona Lisa had quite a crowd, but it was worth the wait!

Notre-Dame Cathedral

After the Louvre, I visited Notre-Dame Cathedral. The Gothic architecture was stunning, and I spent time admiring the intricate details of the facade. The restoration work is progressing well, and it's amazing to see the dedication to preserving this historic landmark.

Evening at Montmartre

Ended the day in Montmartre, where I enjoyed the artistic atmosphere and the beautiful view of Paris from the Sacré-Cœur Basilica. The street artists and musicians added to the vibrant atmosphere.
